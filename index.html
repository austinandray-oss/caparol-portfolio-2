Detailed Project Description

Background

The client sought a calm, daylight-optimized home with durable, low-maintenance finishes suitable for family living and frequent hosting.

Design / Color Concept

We selected a warm neutral spectrum with a subtle contrast wall to anchor the courtyard views. Finishes emphasize texture: matte interior walls for softness and mineral coatings outdoors for longevity.

Execution Process

Surface preparation included priming and micro-filling for seamless planes. Low-VOC paint systems were applied with airless spray in high-traffic zones, ensuring uniform coverage and faster turnaround.
